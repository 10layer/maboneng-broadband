FAQ for the Maboneng Broadband Self Service Portal

1. I just want Wifi - how do I get connected really quickly ?
2. What is the difference between an Account, a Service and a Top-up ?
3. What else can I do on the Portal ?
4. What are the different kind of Services I can get ?
5. What Services can I get where ?
6. How do I get support ?
 
Answers:
1. I just want Wifi - how do I get connected really quickly ?
Either goto the Infotainer and purchase a Voucher and login or,
Have your credit or debit card ready and click on Get Wifi. You will be taken through an express signup process where we will create an Account and a Wifi Service for you. The username for you Account and your Service will be the same. Pay with your card and Login to the Internet using the username and password you have just created.
 
2. What is the difference between an Account, a Service and a Top-up ?
An Account is your contact details, username and password for all Services you subscribe to. You can purchase one or more Service within your account each with their own username and password. A Top-up is a means of adding more Data to your existing Service once you have run out.
 
3. What else can I do on the Portal ?
The Portal is designed to register an Account and select one or more Service to be used within the Maboneng Precinct. Some of the Services are offered directly by Maboneng Broadband, while others are offered by third parties. These Services include Fiber Services from Neotel and Voice services from Voice and Data (Pty) Ltd.
 
The longterm idea is that the Portal becomes the central place for registering and selecting both own Services and third party services. This is going to require some integration work with our third party suppliers.
 
4. What are the different kind of Services I can get ?
We offer either directly or through third parties a variety of Services. Wifi access wherever you see the Maboneng-Wifi SSID
 
5. What Services can I get where ?
Wifi: Main Street Life, Fox Street outside Main Street Life, Kruger Street outside Fox Street Studios.
Fiber: Mainchange, Revolution House and Fox Street Studios, Arts on Main
Fiber with Ethernet last mile: Mainchange, Arts on Main, Fox Street Studios, Revolution House
 
6. How do I contact support ?
The easieyst and most effective way is to email support@mabonengbroadband.com with your username, url and screen grabs of the page where you are experiencing the issue.
